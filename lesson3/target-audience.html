Target Audience

The target audience for the Galveston Chamber of Commerce website are business owners who are interested in engaging with eachother in unified efforts to increase business and give back to the community. Residents and visitors may also be attracted to the site to stay up to date on local events and community intiatives offered by local businesses.

Personas


Lori Smith, Small Business Owner

Lori is a 37 year old married mother of 4 children with some college education. She is working to grow her business and ensure she delivers high quality surfboards, beach kites, and bicycles to beachgoers. She is comfortable talking with customers and building relationships within the local community or those visiting the city. Lori accesses the chamber site on her ipad while putting her youngest child to bed.

Scenarios

The city is planning an event for Spring Break. Lori would like to create a vendor fair at the beach since families will be visiting from all over and may not see her shop. She is hoping to rally other small business to participate in the vendor fair. Her plan is to rent as many of her beach rentals as possible at this event.


Dave wants to know when small businesses move to town so he can get a vending machine or delivery to that new retailer. A new sandwich shop is opening soon and he would like them to offer Chippy Chips exclusively.
